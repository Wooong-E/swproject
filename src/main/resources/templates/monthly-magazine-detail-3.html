한국에서 01. 시장과 마트

시장과 마트에서 사용할 수 있는
현지 예절 및 간단한 한국어 표현

Hello!
Here is a combined guide with useful etiquette
and Korean phrases for your visit to Gyeongsan.

예절 (Local Etiquette)

Greetings: Politeness is key in Korea.
A simple "Hello"
안녕하세요 Annyeonghaseyo
and "Thank you"
감사합니다 Gamsahamnida
are highly appreciated.

Queuing (Lining up): Please wait in line
when checking out at E-mart or waiting
for food at popular stalls in the night market.

Paying: When giving or receiving money
or credit cards, especially to elders,
it is polite to use two hands, or to support
your right forearm with your left hand.

Quiet Zones: The 2nd floor of Starfield Market is
a "Book Ground" and community lounge area
with a bookstore and Starbucks.
Please keep your voice low and be mindful of
others reading in these designated quiet spaces.

Public Seating: After eating at the public tables
in the night market, please clean up
after yourself by disposing of all your trash
(containers, cans, etc.) in the designated bins.

유용한 한국어 (Useful Korean Phrases)

01

화장실 어디예요?
Pronunciation: Hwa-jang-shil eo-di-ye-yo?
Meaning: Where is the restroom?

02

이거 얼마예요?
Pronunciation: I-geo eol-ma-ye-yo?
Meaning: How much is this?

03

이거 하나 주세요.
Pronunciation: I-geo ha-na ju-se-yo.
Meaning: Please give me one of this.

04

계산 부탁드립니다.
Pronunciation: Gye-san bu-tak-deu-rib-ni-da.
Kyeh-sahn boo-tahk-duh-reem-nee-dah.
Meaning: Check, please. / I'd like to pay.

05

OO는 어디에 있는지 알려주실 수 있나요?
Pronunciation: OO-neun eo-di-e it-neun-ji
al-lyeo-ju-sil su in-na-yo?
Meaning:Could you tell me where OO is?
